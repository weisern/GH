Domain: This project is an Online DVD Store that allows customers to purchase DVDs online.
We have limited the features of this store in order to reduce the complexity to one appropiate to this project.
Data:
  • Initial customers will be populated manually by INSERT SQL-statements.
  • Some DVDs will be be populated via SQL-statements, and others via the receipt-in goods form.
  • The actors & directors will be populated at the same time that DVDs are entered.
  • Orders and shopping carts will be populated via the store's interface - ie: we will go shopping :)
    • Queries:
    1. Select all DVD titles that star Tom Hanks.
    2. Select all DVD titles and pictures that are in the genre of Science Fiction.
    3. Select all DVD titles that start with the letter M
    4. Show me the top 5 profitable DVDs (ie DVDs that have the highest profit per unit).
    5. Show me our best 5 customers (customers that have spent the most at our store).
    6. Show me the status of our warehouse and highlight the DVDs that have less than 5 items in stock.
    7. How many customers do we have that live in Victoria?
    8. How many customers dow we have that are between 15 to 20 years old?
    9. What DVDs are currently in my basket (shopping trolley)?
    10. Which DVD title is the most popular (sold the most titles)?
    Reports: There 7 reports available in the Admin Interface:
    1. Top 5 Profitable DVDs - This report lists top 5 DVDs that have the highest per unit profit (the sell price minus the cost)
    2. Stock Sales to Date - This is a naive "Profit & Loss" statement. It itemises each title and compares how many items we have sold with how many items we have in stock.
    3. Stock Status - This tells teh user how much stock there is of each item. Items with less than 5 stock are highlighted yellow and items with no stock are highlighted red.
    4. Popular DVDs - Lists the top 10 popular DVDs (DVDs are ranked popular by how many have been purchased).
    5. Customer Demographics - Shows the demographics of the customers by state (ie: VIC, NSW, etc) and age group.
    6. Popular Genres - Lists the top 10 popular genres (also ranked by how many DVDs have been bought). Note: that since DVDs can have multiple Genres, the total figure may be more than the total number of DVDs actually purchased.
    7. Best Customers - Lists the five best customers ranked by how much they have spent.
    There are two "views" or interfaces available - The Customer interface and the Admin interface.
    The customer interface allows users to create their own account, log in, search for DVDs, add DVDs to their basket (or shopping trolley) and checkout (or purchase) the DVDs in their basket. It also allows them to edit their own profile (name, address, credit card details, etc).
    The admin interface allows the admin to log in (password is quasar), add/edit/delete DVDs, add/edit/delete actors, add/edit/delete directors, add/edit/delete Genres, view reports and import XML data into the database.
    As a side note, the application won't let the admin user delete dvds that have been purchased/in baskets (but they can deactivate them). It also won't let him delete genres,actors or directors that are associated with at least one DVD.
